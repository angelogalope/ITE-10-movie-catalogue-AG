Tom Holland

Thomas Stanley Holland is an English actor. A graduate of the BRIT School for
Performing Arts and Technology in London, he had begun his acting career on the
West End stage in the title role of Billy Elliot the Musical at the Victoria Palace
Theatre from 2008 to 2010.

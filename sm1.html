"SPIDER-MAN"

"Spider-Man" centers on student Peter Parker (Tobey Maguire) who,
after being bitten by a genetically-altered spider, gains superhuman strength and the spider-like ability
to cling to any surface. He vows to use his abilities to fight crime, coming to understand the words of his beloved Uncle Ben: "With great power comes great responsibility."
According to social media posts, this Spider-man movie is Tobey Maguire's best Spider-man film.
It was Tobey Maguire's first Spider-man movie.
